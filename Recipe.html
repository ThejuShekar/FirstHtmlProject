Chocolate Tea

Have you ever tasted chocolate tea? During college days I used to give chocolate tea powder packets to my friends
whenever I travels to my native place Kerala.We used to love chocolate tea.
So today I am providing you the recipe to make a nice chocolate tea. Enjoy!

Chocolate Tea Ingredients

  • Boiled Milk – 1 cup
  • Water-1/2 cup
  • Tea powder – 1 tsp
  • Coco powder 1/2 tsp
  • Sugar – 1 tsp
  • Cinnamon – a small piece

Method

First, you need to boil the Water. After 3 minutes, add sugar,tea powder and Cinnamon.Once it strats boiling,
you can add boiled milk. Keep it in medium flame. After 1 minute, add sugar and Coco powder and stire it well. You can boil the tea in
low flame for another 2 minutes. You can switch off the flame and enjoy the Chocolate Tea.
